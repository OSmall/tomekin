---

Use this workflow for Commander/EDH deck-building:

1. Check reference-data readiness.
2. Draft and confirm a Deck Building Brief.
3. Search local card identities and tags for packages, mana, interaction, protection, card advantage, and win paths.
4. Resolve final names to Card Identity IDs.
5. Validate Commander legality and evaluate the candidate.
6. Render stable Markdown and the strict Portable Decklist.
7. Save the Deck Candidate only after final review.

The Collection is empty in this slice. State that all cards are Missing Cards and do not produce a real Collection Pull List.
